About this project

Building trust, sharing minds

The app will be used by users to vote in hyper-local projects/polls. These polls can be Official polls like "Orçamentos participativos”, Student Body elections, Public works, created by verified accounts like City Halls, and Unofficial polls, created by “civilians” and not Institutions, for getting more visibility for requests to the Mayor’s office, getting opinions from people that live/work in the same area on a specific topic regarding this space, etc.

One of the main goals is also to give access to information on these local projects, and to provide a safe environment for discussion, inhibiting the current polarisation culture in place.
